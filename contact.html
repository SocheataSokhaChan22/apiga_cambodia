Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our programs and organization.

What is the duration of APIGA Cambodia programs?

Our flagship capacity building program typically runs for 6-8 weeks, including intensive workshops, online sessions, and practical assignments. Shorter specialized workshops may run for 2-3 days.

Is there a cost to participate in APIGA programs?

APIGA Cambodia programs are offered free of charge to selected participants thanks to support from ICANN and our partner organizations. This includes training materials, venue costs, and refreshments.

What are the language requirements?

All programs are conducted in English and Khmer. Participants should have proficiency in at least one of these languages. Translation support is available for key sessions when needed.

Do you provide certificates?

Yes, participants who successfully complete our programs receive certificates of completion. These are recognized by ICANN and can be valuable for professional development in digital policy fields.

How can organizations partner with APIGA Cambodia?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our mission. Partners can contribute through venue provision, expert speakers, funding support, or co-developing specialized content. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities.
