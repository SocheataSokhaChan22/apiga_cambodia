About APIGA Cambodia

Building capacity in Internet governance and digital policy to empower Cambodia's digital transformation journey.

About the Program

APIGA Cambodia is an intensive 2-day capacity building program designed to empower professionals across Cambodia with comprehensive knowledge and practical skills in Internet governance, digital policy, and multistakeholder engagement.

Our program brings together participants from government agencies, private sector companies, civil society organizations, and academic institutions to foster cross-sector collaboration and understanding.

Through interactive workshops, expert presentations, and hands-on activities, participants gain the tools and knowledge needed to actively contribute to Cambodia's digital transformation and Internet governance discussions.

Eligibility Criteria

We welcome applications from professionals across various sectors who are passionate about contributing to Cambodia's digital future.

Professional Background

  • ✓ Government officials working in telecommunications, ICT, digital policy, or related areas
  • ✓ Private sector professionals from telecommunications, technology, or digital services companies
  • ✓ Civil society representatives working on digital rights, policy advocacy, or community development
  • ✓ Academic researchers and students in relevant fields

Requirements

  • ✓ Minimum 2 years of relevant professional experience
  • ✓ Strong interest in Internet governance and digital policy
  • ✓ Ability to attend both days of the program
  • ✓ Commitment to apply learned knowledge in professional work
  • ✓ Basic English proficiency (materials provided in both English and Khmer)

What to Expect

Our intensive 2-day program is designed to maximize learning and networking opportunities for all participants.

1

Foundation & Context

Start with fundamental concepts of Internet governance, Cambodia's digital landscape, and the multistakeholder model. Understand the policy framework and key institutions.

2

Deep Dive Sessions

Explore specific areas including DNS management, cybersecurity policy, digital economy frameworks, and emerging technologies like AI governance.

3

Interactive Workshops

Participate in group exercises, policy simulations, and collaborative problem-solving activities that mirror real-world challenges.

4

Networking & Collaboration

Build lasting professional relationships across sectors and develop action plans for future collaboration and knowledge sharing.
