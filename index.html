Objectives

APIGA Cambodia aims to scale for a larger cohort of youth leaders at the local level, focusing on immersive and hands-on learning of Internet governance topics while integrating local topics of interest.

IG Fundamentals

Build strong foundation in Internet governance principles and multistakeholder processes through interactive learning.

Local Network Building

Foster local IG talent retention and build stronger youth networks with local mentors and leaders.

Regional Pathway

Select participants gain direct admission to Regional APIGA in South Korea for advanced leadership development.

Our Supporting Organizations

APIGA Cambodia is organized by Youth IGF Cambodia, Open Development Cambodia, and the Ministry of Posts and Telecommunications, with support from ICANN.
